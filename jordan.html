Jordan: History, Culture & Cuisine

Jordan, a country rich in history, culture, and culinary delights. From the ancient city of Petra to the Dead Sea, Jordan offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

← Back to Blog

Highlights

  • Ancient wonders — legendary archaeological sites and desert monuments.
  • Desert adventures — golden sands, rock formations and stargazing.
  • Dead Sea — unique floating experience and mineral-rich mud.
  • Local hospitality — warm welcomes and traditional Bedouin culture.

Practical tips: wear comfortable shoes for archaeological sites, carry cash for remote areas, and plan desert tours with licensed guides.

Plan a trip

Suggested 3-day itinerary: Day 1 — archaeological highlights; Day 2 — desert tour; Day 3 — Dead Sea relaxation.
